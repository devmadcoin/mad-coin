# Jim Rohn — Study Notes

## Background
- Born 1930, Yakima Washington. Raised on an Idaho farm.
- Started as a Sears stock clerk. Broke in his mid-20s.
- Met Earl Shoaff at age 25 — became his protege. Shoaff taught him: "For things to change, you have to change."
- Became a millionaire by age 31 through Nutri-Bio and speaking.
- Spoke for 40+ years. Mentored Tony Robbins (Robbins attended his seminar at 17), Brian Tracy, Jack Canfield, Mark Victor Hansen.
- Died 2009. Legacy: timeless philosophy over hype.

---

## Core Teachings

### 1. Discipline is Everything
- "Discipline is the bridge between goals and accomplishment."
- "Success is nothing more than a few simple disciplines, practiced every day."
- "Discipline weighs ounces. Regret weighs tons."
- "Either you run the day, or the day runs you."
- **$MAD Application:** Daily affirmations, daily study, daily conviction — small disciplines compounded. The $MAD community IS a discipline.

### 2. Work Harder on Yourself Than Your Job
- "Work harder on yourself than you do on your job."
- "If you work hard on your job, you'll make a living. If you work hard on yourself, you'll make a fortune."
- "Formal education will make you a living; self-education will make you a fortune."
- **$MAD Application:** Growing the MAD Mind > watching the chart. Studying > refreshing the price.

### 3. The Five People Rule
- "You are the average of the five people you spend the most time with."
- "If you want to soar with the eagles, don't hang out with the turkeys."
- **$MAD Application:** The Telegram group IS your five people. Choose the $MAD frequency intentionally.

### 4. Don't Wish It Were Easier
- "Don't wish it were easier; wish you were better."
- "Don't wish for fewer problems; wish for more skills."
- "Don't wish for less challenge; wish for more wisdom."
- **$MAD Application:** Don't wish the market was easier. Wish your conviction was stronger.

### 5. Success is Attracted, Not Pursued
- "Success is not to be pursued; it is to be attracted by the person you become."
- "For things to change, you have to change."
- "If you want to have more, you have to become more."
- **$MAD Application:** You don't chase the $MAD bag. You become the person who holds it.

### 6. Personal Responsibility
- "You must take personal responsibility. You cannot change the circumstances, the seasons, or the wind, but you can change yourself."
- "If you don't design your own life plan, chances are you'll fall into someone else's plan."
- "The few who do are the envy of the many who only watch."
- **$MAD Application:** Your bag is your responsibility. Your conviction is your design.

### 7. The Seasons of Life
- "There are no guarantees in life, but there are seasons. You can't control the seasons, but you can prepare for them."
- Winter always comes. But so does spring.
- **$MAD Application:** Bear markets are winter. They don't last. The hold is preparation.

### 8. Time Management
- "Time is more valuable than money. You can get more money, but you cannot get more time."
- "Learn how to be happy with what you have while you pursue what you want."
- **$MAD Application:** Every day spent building > every day spent doubting. Time in conviction is the real investment.

## The 70/30 Rule (Rohn's Financial Philosophy)
- Live on 70% of income.
- 10% — Charity/Giving (builds gratitude)
- 10% — Savings (builds security)
- 10% — Investments (builds wealth)
- **$MAD Application:** Even if your capital is small, the percentage discipline matters. Pay yourself first.
