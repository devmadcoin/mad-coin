## The Economic Hit Man (EHM) System

### Core Mechanism
Economic hit men are **highly paid professionals who cheat countries out of trillions of dollars** by funneling World Bank, USAID, and "aid" money into US corporations and wealthy families controlling natural resources.

### The Playbook
1. **Inflated projections**: Create fraudulent economic forecasts showing massive growth from infrastructure projects
2. **Enormous loans**: Convince developing nations to accept loans they can never repay
3. **US company contracts**: Loans require hiring US firms (Halliburton, Bechtel, Brown & Root)
4. **Debt trap**: Countries default, become politically neutered, forced to serve US interests
5. **Wealth extraction**: Top 1% in these nations capture 70-90% of wealth while citizens suffer

### Tools of the EHM
- Fraudulent financial reports
- Rigged elections
- Payoffs and extortion
- Sex and murder (when needed)
- "Aid" disguised as debt slavery

### The Jackal (Plan B)
When EHMs fail, "jackals" are sent — CIA-backed operatives who overthrow or assassinate leaders who resist (e.g., Panama's Omar Torrijos, Ecuador's Jaime Roldós).

### Corporatocracy
Perkins coined this term: a system where corporations, banks, and governments collude to control the world through economic coercion rather than military force. It's "a game as old as empire, but with terrifying new dimensions."

## The Death Economy vs. Life Economy

### Death Economy
Perkins' term for the current global system:
- **Based on**: Wars, resource extraction, debt slavery, environmental destruction
- **Grows by**: Ravaging the earth, exploiting the poor, creating scarcity
- **Destroys**: Its own resources at accelerating rates
- **Examples**: Oil dependency, planned obsolescence, speculative finance, predatory lending
- **Debt as prison**: "This book offers a startling reminder that debt is the new form of prison."

### Life Economy
Perkins' proposed alternative:
- **Based on**: Cleaning pollution, renewable energy, feeding the hungry, regenerative practices
- **Grows by**: Creating abundance, solving real problems, building sustainable systems
- **Renews**: Resources and communities rather than depleting them
- **Examples**: Green tech, circular economy, local food systems, community banking
- **Core insight**: The 1% have created a death economy. The 99% must create a life economy.

### The Consciousness Revolution
Perkins: "We are living at the time of the greatest revolution in history, the Consciousness Revolution. People around the world are waking up to the fact that we must transition from a Death Economy to a Life Economy."

## Indigenous Wisdom & Shamanism

### From EHM to Shaman
Perkins' transformation wasn't intellectual — it was shamanic. After seeing the destruction his work caused in Ecuador and Indonesia, he returned to the Amazon and was trained by indigenous shamans.

### Key Concepts

**Touching the Jaguar**
Amazonian concept: face your greatest fear ("the jaguar") and transform it into power. Perkins: "The shaman taught me to touch the jaguar — to transform fears into positive action." Applied to personal and global transformation.

**The World Is As You Dream It**
Indigenous wisdom: reality manifests from collective dreams/visions. "If you want to change the world, you have to change your dream." The modern industrial world dreamed convenience and created gridlock, pollution, and destruction.

**Shapeshifting**
Ancient shamanic practice of transforming oneself to achieve unity with all things by "becoming the other." Mental techniques to merge with other living creatures and transform personal reality. Perkins applied this to global transformation — shapeshifting the death economy into a life economy.

**Psychonavigation**
Traveling outside the body through visions and dream wanderings to access inner guidance. Still practiced by tribal cultures worldwide.

### The Condor and the Eagle Prophecy
Andean prophecy: Condor (heart/intuition/indigenous) and Eagle (mind/technology/modern) must fly together. For 500 years the Eagle dominated. Now the Condor is rising. The future requires both.
