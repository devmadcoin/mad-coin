### 3. TikTok Algorithm 2026
**Status:** Deep research complete, strategy ready

**Key Findings (from multiple sources):**
- **Follower-first testing** (2026 change): New videos shown to followers first before non-followers
- **Completion rate bar raised to ~70%** (up from ~50% in 2024)
- **Shares and saves now outweigh likes** — deep engagement prioritized
- **3-second rule**: Hook viewers in first 3 seconds or lose them
- **Optimal posting**: 3-5x per week, consistency > volume
- **Hashtag strategy**: 3-5 highly relevant hashtags, not stuffing
- **Caption keywords**: Matter more than hashtags for discovery (+20-40% visibility)
- **Video length**: 11-18s for virality, 15-30s for engagement, 60s+ for monetization
- **Original audio**: TikTok rewards original sounds over borrowed ones
- **Content mix**: 70% value/entertainment, 20% interactive, 10% promotional

**$MAD Application:**

**Status:** Pipeline researched, ready to begin execution

**Key Findings:**
