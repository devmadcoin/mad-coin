# The Laws of Human Nature — $MAD Study

**Author:** Robert Greene  
**Published:** 2018  
**Core Premise:** Humans are not rational. We are driven by irrational emotions, unconscious biases, and ancient evolutionary patterns. Master these laws and you master human behavior.

---

## 18 Laws at a Glance

### Self-Mastery (Laws 1–5)
1. **Law of Irrationality** — Master your emotional self. Emotions precede conscious thought. We feel first, then justify.  
   *Biases:* confirmation, conviction, appearance, group, superiority, blame.  
   *Strategy:* Observe yourself under stress. Record. Wait. Channel emotional energy.
2. **Law of Narcissism** — Everyone is narcissistic to a degree. Transform self-love into empathy. Healthy narcissism builds; unhealthy destroys.
3. **Law of Role-playing** — See through people’s masks. Master the three skills: reading the second language, decoding keys, impression management.
4. **Law of Compulsive Behavior** — Past behavior predicts future behavior. Look at track record, not promises. Character is pattern.
5. **Law of Covetousness** — Humans want what they don’t have. Make yourself an elusive object of desire. Mystery creates value.

### Perception & Influence (Laws 6–10)
6. **Law of Shortsightedness** — Step back. See the big picture. Reacting to immediate stimuli is the default trap of human nature.
7. **Law of Defensiveness** — Don’t challenge people directly. Confirm their self-opinion. Soften resistance. Align your idea with their identity.
8. **Law of Self-sabotage** — Your attitude shapes your situation. Constricted attitudes create negative loops. Expansive attitudes create opportunity.
9. **Law of Repression** — Confront your dark side. Integrate it, or it will leak and destroy you. The shadow must be owned, not hidden.
10. **Law of Envy** — Beware the fragile ego. Envy is universal, denied, and dangerous. Decode it early. Diffuse it. Build your own self-worth.

### Character & Purpose (Laws 11–15)
11. **Law of Grandiosity** — Know your limits. Success breeds delusion. Stay grounded. Practical grandiosity = confidence without delusion.
12. **Law of Gender Rigidity** — Every human has masculine and feminine traits. Rigidity limits you. Flexibility unlocks full potential.
13. **Law of Aimlessness** — Advance with purpose. Without purpose, you drift. With purpose, you become magnetic and unstoppable.
14. **Law of Conformity** — Resist the group’s downward pull. Know how groups influence you. Build groups with an *upward* pull.
15. **Law of Fickleness** — People are ambivalent. Make them want to follow you. Authority is earned, not claimed. Inner authority > title.

### Power & Mortality (Laws 16–18)
16. **Law of Aggression** — See hostility behind the friendly façade. Everyone has aggressive energy. Channel it productively. Watch for passive-aggression.
17. **Law of Generational Myopia** — Seize the historical moment. You are defined by your generation’s spirit. Understand cycles. Exploit the zeitgeist.
18. **Law of Death Denial** — Contemplate mortality. Death awareness creates urgency, clarity, and courage. Denying death makes you soft and distracted.

## Core Insight: The Irrationality Engine

Greene’s foundational insight:  
**Humans are emotional creatures who use logic to justify decisions they already made emotionally.**

You cannot argue someone out of an emotional position. You must shift their emotional state first.
