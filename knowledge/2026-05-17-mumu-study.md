**Contract:** `0xec3dc9ba0226dd571786767bfc8df91e045cdc82`  
**Website:** https://mumuogeth.com/  
**Status:** Community Takeover (CTO) — no team, no dev wallet  
**Network:** Ethereum (ERC-20)  
**Deployed:** April 17, 2023

### Origin Story
- Born on **4chan's /biz/ in 2018** as the eternal bullish market mascot
- Organic internet creation — not owned or controlled by anyone
- Personification of bullish market sentiment
- Part of the meme pantheon alongside Pepe, Wojak, and Apu Apustaja
- Eternal rivalry with **Bobo the Bear** (bears) and **Snibs** (crab market)
- The OG deployment, not a copy

### Tokenomics
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Supply | 1,000,000,000 |
| Burned So Far | ~40% |
| Buy/Sell Tax | 0% / 0% |
| Burn Rate | 2.5% per LP burn trigger |
| Holders | 235+ |

### Unique LP Burn Mechanism
- **No transfer tax. No wallet tax.**
- Burns happen directly from the liquidity pool
- Anyone can trigger a burn by sending **1 token** to the LP address
- Contract removes MUMU from pool, sends to dead address
- Same ETH, fewer tokens = price adjusts upward
- Only LP tokens burned. Your holdings are never touched.

### Vitalik Connection
- **Vitalik Buterin holds 10%** of total supply (100,000,000 MUMU)
- In wallet `vitalik.eth` since early days
- Verified on Etherscan

### Community

- Telegram + X presence
- "Not financial advice" standard disclaimer
- Emphasis on: OG status, no tax, community governance
