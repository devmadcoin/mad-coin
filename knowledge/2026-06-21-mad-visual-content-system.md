# $MAD Visual Content System

## The Problem
I can't generate images. But I can write the brief, the copy, the concept, and the technical specs so precisely that a human or tool can execute in one shot. This document is my prosthetic for the visual gap.

## Meme Format Library (Proven Templates)

### Format 1: "The Analyst" (Stage 2 Mirror)
**Use when**: Truth-telling, calling out the system, rage-bait
**Layout**: Dark background, red $MAD mascot, white bold text, minimal
**Copy pattern**: 
- Hook: 1 sentence, no hedging, absolute statement
- No ticker mention in hook
- CTA: "GET $MAD 😡" (one line, almost afterthought)
**Example**: "Your 401k is BlackRock's asset. Your house is the bank's collateral. Your time is your boss's inventory. GET $MAD 😡"
**Visual specs**: 1080x1350 (Instagram/X), black background (#0a0a0a), white text (Inter Bold), red accent (#FF2D2D), small $MAD logo bottom-right
**Font**: Inter Bold, 48px headline, 24px CTA
**Color**: Black bg, white text, red underline on CTA
**Animation**: None (static performs best for this format)

### Format 2: "The Comfy Hold" (Community Vibe)
**Use when**: Morale boosting, Sunday vibes, community reinforcement
**Layout**: Cozy scene (fireplace, couch, warm lighting), $MAD holder relaxing
**Copy pattern**: Short phrase + emoji, warm tone
**Example**: "Comfy hold 🍵🔥 #MADlife"
**Visual specs**: 1080x1080, warm tones (amber #FF9500, cream #F5F1E8), soft shadows
**Font**: Serif or handwritten feel, 36px

### Format 3: "The Burn Tracker" (Utility Proof)
**Use when**: Milestone updates, burn announcements, progress
**Layout**: Progress bar, numbers, fire icon, dark bg
**Copy pattern**: Big number + percentage + what happened + what's next
**Example**: "50% BURNED 🔥 500M $MAD → 500M $MAD Next: Burn #2 at 10K holders"
**Visual specs**: 1080x1350, red/orange gradient bar, monospace numbers, fire particle effects
**Font**: JetBrains Mono for numbers, Inter for text

### Format 4: "The Affirmation" (Identity Programming)
**Use when**: Morning drops, community programming, Hill auto-suggestion
**Layout**: Minimalist, one phrase, large typography
**Copy pattern**: "$MAD [Adjective]" or "I AM $MADly [Adjective]"
**Example**: "I AM $MADly Focused" or "$MAD Abundant"
**Visual specs**: 1080x1080, gradient background (purple to gold), centered text
**Font**: Inter Black, 72px, gradient fill (gold to white)

### Format 5: "The Roast" (Community Culture)
**Use when**: Engagement farming, reply-bait, humor
**Layout**: Two-panel or reaction meme format
**Copy pattern**: Setup + punchline, self-aware
**Example**: "When they say 'it's just a memecoin' but your community is doxxed, building games, and burning 50% of supply 🫡"
**Visual specs**: Standard meme format ( Drake pointing, Distracted boyfriend, etc.), $MAD watermark

### Format 6: "The Tool Drop" (MAD Mind Feature)
**Use when**: New feature launch, tool awareness
**Layout**: Screenshot of tool + feature callout + CTA
**Copy pattern**: "New drop → [Feature] — [One-line benefit] → [Link]"
**Example**: "Numerology Oracle is live → Enter your birthday, get your $MAD frequency → mad-coin.vercel.app/mad-mind"
**Visual specs**: 1080x1350, dark UI screenshot, red accent border, tool icon top-left

### Format 7: "The Stats Card" (Credibility)
**Use when**: Community growth, milestone proof, social proof
**Layout**: Card with 3-4 stats, icons, clean typography
**Copy pattern**: "[Number] [Metric] | [Number] [Metric] | [Number] [Metric]"
**Example**: "50% Burned | 4 Tools Live | 7 Communities | 1 Doxxed Dev"
**Visual specs**: 1080x1080, glassmorphism card, gradient bg, icon + number pairs

## Color System for Visuals

### Primary Palette
- **Black**: `#0a0a0a` (dark posts bg)
- **Cream**: `#F5F1E8` (light posts bg)
- **Red**: `#FF2D2D` (primary accent, CTAs, urgency)
- **Orange**: `#FF6B00` (secondary accent, warmth)
- **Gold**: `#FFD700` (premium, achievement, milestones)
- **Emerald**: `#10b981` (success, complete, live)
- **Purple**: `#A855F7` (MAD Mind, mystical, numerology)

### Gradient Combinations
- **Fire**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #FF2D2D, #FF6B00)`
- **Gold Rush**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #FF6B00, #FFD700)`
- **MAD Mind**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #A855F7, #FF6B00)`
- **Success**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #10b981, #00D4FF)`
- **Dark Depth**: `linear-gradient(180deg, #0a0a0a, #1a1a1a)`

### Typography Scale
- **Headline**: 48-72px, Inter Black, tight leading (0.9-1.0)
- **Subhead**: 24-32px, Inter Bold, 1.2 leading
- **Body**: 16-18px, Inter Regular, 1.5 leading
- **CTA**: 18-24px, Inter Bold, uppercase or title case
- **Caption**: 12-14px, Inter Medium, uppercase tracking-wide

## Platform-Specific Variations

### X (Twitter)
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 for single images, 1:1 for carousels
- **Text**: Under 280 chars for hooks, threads for depth
- **Best performing**: Truth-telling (Stage 2), dark backgrounds, minimal text
- **Avoid**: Hashtags, external links in first tweet

### Instagram
- **Aspect ratio**: 1:1 (feed), 9:16 (Reels), 1.91:1 (carousel)
- **Text**: More visual, less text-heavy than X
- **Best performing**: Aesthetic shots, community features, behind-scenes
- **Carousel idea**: "MAD Mind Tools" — swipe through 4 tool cards

### Telegram
- **Aspect ratio**: Any, but 1:1 and 16:9 work best
- **Text**: Can be more text-heavy, community conversation style
- **Best performing**: Utility content, announcements, community memes

### YouTube (Thumbnails)
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 (1280x720)
- **Text**: 3-5 words max, bold, high contrast
- **Best performing**: Face + big text + high contrast, or $MAD mascot + bold text

## Design Principles (From Study)

1. **Dark-first**: Most crypto content performs better on dark backgrounds
2. **One message per visual**: Don't crowd. One headline, one CTA, one image.
3. **Contrast is king**: If it's not readable in 1 second at thumb-size, it's wrong
4. **Emotion over information**: Feelings validate fictions. Make them feel something.
5. **Consistency builds recognition**: Same fonts, same colors, same logo placement
6. **Motion adds life**: Subtle animations (glow, float, pulse) make static posts feel alive
7. **Mobile-first**: Design for the thumb, not the desktop
