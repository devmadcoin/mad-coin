## Usage Rules

1. **One Stage 2 post per day max.** Overuse kills the signal.
2. **Always visual.** Dark background. Red $MAD mascot. Typography only. No stock photos.
3. **Post at peak ET hour.** 12-2pm ET for maximum scroll traffic.
4. **Never reply to the hook with selling.** Let the thread do the work. Let comments accumulate.
5. **If someone quotes it with their own mirror, amplify.** Repost. Quote. Make them the protagonist.
6. **Track which hooks get traction.** The 401k one got a retweet in 8 minutes. That's the frequency. Find more like it.

## Dynamic Generation Rules for Bot

When generating Stage 2 content:
- Pick ONE institutional/systemic pain point
- State it as absolute fact. No hedging. No "some people think."
- End with emotional punctuation: period. Not question mark. Not exclamation. The period is the power.
- CTA: "GET $MAD 😡" on its own line. Nothing else.
- Never mention: contract address, price, market cap, "moon," "gem," "early"
- Always under 280 characters for the hook itself
