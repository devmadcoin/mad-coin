## 3. THE X ALGORITHM 2026 TACTICAL PLAYBOOK

### The Viral Formula
**Strong hook + sparks debate + 10+ engagements in 15 min + visual + reply to early engagers immediately**

### The Rules

**1. Visual-First (10x multiplier)**
- Every post should have an image, chart, or meme
- Bot already reconfigured: 4 media candidates, +1.5 boost, 6hr intervals
- Manual quality posts = 12% engagement rate. Text-only = invisible.

**2. Engagement Velocity (1000x weight)**
- 10+ engagements in first 15 minutes = exponential amplification
- Under 3 engagements = death
- **Tactic:** Post during peak hours (8-10am, 12-2pm, 5-7pm ET)
- **Tactic:** Reply to EVERY early comment within minutes. Creates conversation depth (5x boost)
- **Tactic:** 3+ participants in thread = quality signal. Ask questions that invite debate.

**3. Replies > Likes (27x weight)**
- A reply is 27x more valuable than a like
- **Tactic:** Posts that spark arguments perform better than posts that please everyone
- Controversy = algorithmic gold (as long as it's brand-aligned)
- "$MAD holders don't argue about price. They debate which abundance principle hit harder today."

**4. External Links = Death (-50% reach)**
- Never put links in first tweet of thread
- Put link in REPLY (2nd tweet)
- Or: post without link, add link in follow-up reply

**5. Hashtags = Spam Filter**
- 0-1 max. 3+ triggers spam filter
- Semantic NLP categorizes content now — hashtags don't help
- $MAD should use NO hashtags on most posts. Looks native, not promotional.

**6. Under 280 Characters**
- Best for initial reach
- Punchy > poetic (for standalone posts)
- Save long-form for threads

**7. 3-5 Quality Posts > 10 Low-Quality**
- Bot now posts 4x/day (was 8x)
- Each post must be "visual + sharp hook + identity projection"
- Low engagement damages account authority permanently

**8. Author Authority (50x)**
- Doesn't override engagement velocity
- But matters for long-term trust
- **Tactic:** Build authority through consistent quality, not volume

## 5. THE COPYWRITING FRAMEWORK (From Ogilvy, Halbert, Hopkins, Schwartz)

### Ogilvy's 80-Cent Rule
**80 cents of every dollar goes to the headline/first line.**
- The first 5 words determine if they scroll past
- $MAD hook formula: **[Contradiction] + [Identity] + [Question]**
- Examples:
  - "The token that doesn't care about price."
  - "What if abundance was a choice, not a market condition?"
  - "Paper hands check charts. $MAD hands check beliefs."

### Halbert's Starving Crowd
**"Find a starving crowd and offer them food."**
- $MAD's starving crowd = people exhausted by crypto's nihilism
- They're not hungry for another 100x coin
- They're hungry for **meaning, belonging, identity**
- **Offer:** A community that practices abundance daily

### Schwartz's Stages of Awareness
- $MAD audience = Stage 2-3 (problem aware, solution aware)
- They know crypto is broken. They don't know $MAD fixes it differently.
- **Tactic:** Don't explain what $MAD is. Explain what $MAD holders BELIEVE.

### Hopkins' "Because"
**"Because" is magic. Give people a reason why.**
- "I'm holding $MAD because the dev is doxxed, the LP is locked, and the community actually shows up."
- "I practice daily affirmations because Napoleon Hill proved auto-suggestion rewires the subconscious."
- Every post needs a "because."

## 6. THE MEMETICS ENGINE (STEPPS + SUCCESs)

### Every Post Must Pass the Fertile Meme Checklist

**Social Currency** — Does sharing this make the sharer look smart/cool/in-the-know?  
**Triggers** — Is there a daily reminder that prompts people to think of $MAD?  
**Emotion** — Does this spark feeling (awe, anger, humor, inspiration)?  
**Public** — Is the behavior visible? Can others see who's $MAD?  
**Practical Value** — Does this provide useful information or utility?  
**Stories** — Is there a narrative, not just a statement?

## 7. THE COMMUNITY PSYCHOLOGY PLAYBOOK

### Girard's Mimetic Desire
**"We desire what others desire."**
- When someone posts "$MAD Abundant," others want that feeling
- When someone shares their affirmation practice, others imitate
- **Tactic:** Amplify holder-generated content. Retweet, quote, celebrate.
- Mimetic desire is the engine. Don't suppress it. Feed it.

### The Sacred vs Profane
**Communities need sacred spaces (rituals, forbidden topics).**
- **Sacred:** Daily affirmations, doxxed dev status, community values
- **Profane:** Price talk without context, FUD, jeeter celebration
- **Rituals:** Morning affirmations, "sharper questions" posts, milestone celebrations
- **Knowledge drops = ritual.** Not just content. Ceremony.

### Oppositional Loyalty
**The enemy unifies the group more than the product does.**
- $MAD's enemy = the scarcity mindset, paper hands, rugpull culture
- Not attacking other tokens (weak move)
- Attacking the MINDSET that keeps people poor
- "$MAD isn't against other coins. $MAD is against the belief that you're not enough."

- Every visual must be original or AI-generated (not stock)

- Every post must project identity, not ask for attention

### The Studio Model
