### Composition (0.8s, 4 layers)

1. **Deep bass thump** — 80Hz, 0.12s, exponential decay 0.08s (the punch)
2. **FM bell shimmer** — 523Hz C5, FM synthesis (m_ratio=1.5, idx=3.0), metallic ring, 0.35s starting at 20ms (the money sound)
3. **High phonk ping** — 2500Hz burst, 0.06s at 80ms (the edge)
4. **Filtered noise texture** — bandpass noise at 4000Hz, 0.15s at 50ms, very quiet (the body)

### Technical Details
- **FM synthesis** for the bell: `sin(2π·fc·t + β·sin(2π·fm·t))` creates metallic harmonics without needing samples
- **Exponential decay** envelopes: `e^(-t/τ)` for natural acoustic decay
- **Soft clipping**: `tanh(track * 1.2)` for punch without digital distortion
- **Tail fade**: 50ms linear fade to avoid click/pop at end
