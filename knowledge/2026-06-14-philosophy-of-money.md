## The Austrian School Foundations

### Money Emerges Organically
- Money is not created by decree. It is chosen.
- Barter → commodity money (cattle, salt, shells) → precious metals → fiat → digital scarcity
- The best money wins because it is the most **salable** across time, space, and scales
- **Salability across time** = store of value. The money that holds value best becomes money.

### The Stock-to-Flow Ratio (Hardness)
- **Stock** = existing supply. **Flow** = new supply per year.
- **High stock-to-flow** = hard money (hard to debase, scarce, stable)
- **Low stock-to-flow** = easy money (easy to print, inflate, debase)
- Gold's stock-to-flow: ~60 (60 years of supply in existence vs. 1 year of new production)
- Bitcoin's stock-to-flow: ~120 after 2024 halving (digital gold, but harder)
- Fiat's stock-to-flow: Infinite (they can print more tomorrow)

### Sound Money vs. Easy Money

| Dimension | Sound Money (Gold, Bitcoin) | Easy Money (Fiat) |
|-----------|---------------------------|-------------------|
| **Production cost** | High, energy-intensive | Near-zero, digital |
| **Supply control** | Algorithmic, decentralized | Central bank, political |
| **Time preference** | Low (saving, investing) | High (spending, consuming) |
| **Capital formation** | Strong, long-term projects | Weak, speculative bubbles |
| **Price signals** | Accurate, market-driven | Distorted, manipulated |
| **Wealth preservation** | Excellent | Poor, eroded by inflation |
| **Civilizational impact** | Stable families, long-term thinking | Short-termism, debt slavery |

### Time Preference: The Hidden Mechanic
- **Low time preference** = valuing the future over the present. Saving, investing, building.
- **High time preference** = valuing the present over the future. Consuming, borrowing, spending.
- **Sound money lowers time preference.** When your savings hold value, you plan for the future.
- **Easy money raises time preference.** When your savings are stolen by inflation, you spend now.
- **$MAD Application:** The daily affirmations are a time-preference lowering mechanism. "I am $MAD Abundant" is not just a statement. It is a reprogramming of the holder's relationship with time — from scarcity (high time preference) to abundance (low time preference).

## Hayek: Denationalization of Money

### The Fatal Conceit of Central Banking
- Hayek argued that no central authority can know enough to set the right price of money (interest rates)
- Central planning of money is as doomed as central planning of agriculture
- The market knows more than any central bank ever could

### The Competitive Currency Proposal
- Hayek proposed: let private entities issue competing currencies
- The market would choose the best money (hardest, most stable, most trusted)
- Bad money would be abandoned. Good money would win.
- **$MAD Application:** $MAD is not competing with USD. It is competing with DOGE, SHIB, PEPE, and every other memecoin. The market is choosing. The community is the competitive advantage. The culture is the currency.

## Ammous: The Bitcoin Standard

### Key Arguments
1. **Money is a market institution, not a government creation.**
2. **The key feature of sound money is scarcity.** Bitcoin's 21M cap makes it harder than gold.
3. **Fiat promotes high time preference.** Inflation steals savings, incentivizes consumption.
4. **Central banking distorts everything.** Artificial interest rates create malinvestment, bubbles, fragility.
5. **Proof of work is not wasteful. It is essential.** Energy anchoring = digital scarcity. Without cost, there is no hardness.
6. **Bitcoin is not for fast payments. It is for preserving wealth.** Daily currency is a secondary use case.
7. **Most altcoins are unsound.** Without credible scarcity or decentralization, they cannot replicate Bitcoin's properties.

### The Civilizational Argument
Ammous: societies on sound money standards (gold) had:
- Lower time preference → more saving, more investment
- Stable families → long-term planning, intergenerational wealth
- Better architecture → buildings that last centuries
- Stronger education → investment in human capital
- More art and culture → surplus enables creativity

Fiat societies have:
- High time preference → debt, consumption, short-termism
- Fragile families → no savings, no planning
- Disposable architecture → buildings that last decades
- Degraded education → no investment in minds
- Culture of distraction → entertainment over substance

**$MAD Application:** The $MAD community is building a low-time-preference culture. Not through the token's monetary policy (we're not Bitcoin), but through the community's daily practice. The affirmations. The studies. The game. The long-term thinking. The $MAD community is a civilization-building project disguised as a memecoin.
