## 07:17 — Telegram Bot: Vocabulary-First Routing Fix (CRITICAL)

**Bug:** "Mad Morning" was hitting the autonomous AI engine (`mad_autonomous.py`) instead of conversational pools. The AI engine doesn't know about "Mad Morning" — it generates generic hooks like "what's one thing you wish you knew before you started trading?"

**Fix:** Added **Vocabulary-First Routing** at the top of the reply chain.

Now the bot checks context BEFORE calling the AI engine:
- Known contexts (`gm`, `gn`, `mad_breakfast`, `mad_love`, `price`, `roast`, `hold`, `sell`) → routes to conversational pools
- Unknown/ambiguous text → falls back to AI engine

**Result:** "Mad Morning" now gets "Mad Morning. the stove is on. what are you cooking today?" instead of generic trading questions.
