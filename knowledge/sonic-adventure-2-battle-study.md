## GAME OVERVIEW

**Sonic Adventure 2: Battle** (2001, GameCube) — Enhanced port of Dreamcast's SA2. 
The defining game for Chao Garden mechanics, Chao Racing, and Chao Karate.

### Three Gardens:
1. **Neutral Garden** — Unlocked first. Two fruit trees, pond with waterfall, cave to Chao Race. Basic grass/trees/pond.
2. **Hero Garden** — Unlocked when a Chao becomes Hero. Heaven-themed. Clouds, bright light, floating feel.
3. **Dark Garden** — Unlocked when a Chao becomes Dark. Horror-themed. Red tint, skull motifs, darker atmosphere.

## CHAO ANATOMY — THE BLUEPRINT

### Base Chao Body Proportions
**CRITICAL FOR MODELING:**

- **Body shape:** Small, compact, almost egg-like torso. Rounded, NOT angular.
- **Head-to-body ratio:** Head is roughly 40% of total height. Very large head = cute factor.
- **Body height:** Short. Chao are small creatures, roughly knee-high to Sonic.
- **Limbs:** Stubby arms and legs. Arms slightly longer than legs. No elbows/knees visible — simple cylinders with rounded ends.
- **Hands/Feet:** Rounded nubs. No individual fingers/toes. Just rounded balls at limb ends.
- **Wings:** Small, butterfly-like, positioned on upper back. Folded when not in use.
- **Tail:** Tiny nub on rear. Almost invisible.
- **Emotion Ball:** Floating dot above head. Color indicates alignment:
  - Blue = Neutral/Happy
  - Red = Dark/Angry
  - Yellow = Hero
  - Heart = Ready to mate
  - Spiral = Sad/Dying

### Head Details
- **Face area:** Flat-ish front plane with large eyes
- **Eyes:** Very large, oval, expressive. Black pupils with white highlights. 50%+ of face real estate.
- **Mouth:** Simple line or small curve. Can be:
  - No mouth (neutral)
  - V smile (happy)
  - Upside-down V (sad)
  - Frown (angry)
  - Grin (excited)
  - Squiggly (confused)
- **Forehead:** Smooth, rounded dome
- **Head top:** Can have various "jesters" or ornaments based on evolution

### Body Surface
- **Texture:** Smooth, slightly shiny/wet look. Like a polished stone or jellybean.
- **Material:** Plastic-like sheen in SA2. Slightly translucent in some lighting.
- **Color system:** Two-tone — main body color + accent on limbs/feet/head tip.

## CHAO EVOLUTION VISUAL CHANGES

### First Evolution (Adult — Cocoon)
**Determined by alignment + skill focus:**

**ALIGNMENT (Skin/Wings/Tail):**
- **Neutral:** Baby-like appearance. Blue emotion dot. Normal tail.
- **Hero:** White/pale skin. Halo emotion dot. Normal tail. Two head tufts.
- **Dark:** Dark/dusky skin. Spiky red emotion dot. Spiky tail. Zigzag point on head.

**SKILL TYPE (Body Shape):**
- **Normal:** Balanced proportions. Standard Chao shape.
- **Swim:** Fin-like features, webbed feet, streamlines body.
- **Fly:** Larger wings, lighter body, slightly elevated posture.
- **Run:** Leaner, longer legs, athletic build.
- **Power:** Bulkier, muscular, broader shoulders.

### Second Evolution (Continuous — No Cocoon)
- Hybrid forms possible (Swim+Run, Fly+Power, etc.)
- Gradual visual changes based on stat balance
- Head ornaments grow/droop (jesters, crests, spikes)
- Body bulk changes
- Color shifts

### Character Chao (Rare)
- **Sonic Chao:** Neutral/Run. Blue with spiky hair quills.
- **Shadow Chao:** Dark/Run. Black/red with spiked hair.
- **NiGHTS Chao:** Neutral/Fly. Purple with jester hat.
- **Tails/Knuckles/Amy Chao:** Event-only, very rare.

## CHAO BEHAVIOR & PERSONALITY

### Expressions:
- **Happy:** Eyes closed in ^ shape, V smile, bouncing
- **Neutral:** Normal eyes, no mouth, gentle sway
- **Sad:** Downcast eyes, upside-down V mouth, slumped posture
- **Angry:** Narrow eyes, frown, stomping feet
- **Sleeping:** Eyes closed, lying down or sitting upright
- **Eating:** Mouth open, hands near face, happy wiggle
- **Dancing:** Arms up, spinning, eyes closed
- **Evolution:** Sitting still, cocoon forming around body
- **Mating:** Heart emotion dot, flower bed, nuzzle animation

### Movement:
- **Walking:** Short steps, slight waddle, arms swing
- **Running:** Arms pump, faster steps, determined face
- **Swimming:** Dog paddle, floating, splashing
- **Flying:** Brief wing flaps, short hops, not sustained
- **Idle:** Gentle sway, looking around, small hops

## CHAO RACE TRACKS

### Race Types:
- **Beginner:** Simple flat track, basic obstacles
- **Intermediate:** Hurdles, water sections, slight elevation
- **Advanced:** Complex terrain, multiple paths, traps
- **Challenge:** Tricky shortcuts, stat-specific advantages

### Track Elements:
- Starting gates with numbers
- Checkered finish line
- Hurdles to jump
- Water pools (Swim Chao advantage)
- Treadmills (Run Chao advantage)
- Cliffs (Fly Chao advantage)
- Rocks to break (Power Chao advantage)
- Prize pedestals (1st/2nd/3rd place)

## VISUAL STYLE OF SA2:B

### Overall Aesthetic:
- **Era:** Early 2000s 3D — clean polygons, simple textures, bold colors
- **Character design:** Anime-influenced (Yuji Uekawa style)

- **Menus:** Clean, blue/white, geometric

- **Chao stats:** Radars showing Swim/Fly/Run/Power/Stamina

## KEY LESSONS FOR $MAD CHAO MODELING

### Must Capture:
1. **Proportions:** Big head (40%), stubby limbs, compact body
2. **Material:** Smooth, slightly shiny, wet-stone look
3. **Face:** Large expressive eyes, simple mouth line, readable emotion
4. **Emotion Ball:** Floating dot above head, color-coded
5. **Wings:** Small, butterfly-like, folded when idle
6. **Movement:** Waddle-walk, bouncy idle, happy bounce
7. **Personality:** Cute but capable. Not helpless — they race, fight, evolve.

### Must Avoid:
1. **Human proportions:** Chao are NOT human-shaped. Compact/round.
2. **Sharp edges:** Everything is smooth, rounded, organic.
3. **Realistic textures:** Plastic/smooth, not fur/skin.
4. **Complex anatomy:** Simple limbs, no joints visible.
5. **Dark/scary:** Even Dark Chao are cute-evil, not horror.

### $MAD Adaptation:
- **Alignment:** Dark Chao base (matches $MAD energy) but keep cute core
- **Emotion ball:** Red ($MAD color) instead of standard red
- **Accessories:** Add $MAD chain, sunglasses, or other crypto swagger
- **Attitude:** Confident, competitive, slightly cocky like Sonic
- **Race integration:** Chao Race aesthetic with crypto theming

## TECHNICAL MODELING NOTES

### Blender Approach:
- **Base mesh:** UV sphere, heavily squashed vertically for body
- **Head:** Separate UV sphere, 1.5x body width, merged at neck
- **Limbs:** Cylinders with sphere caps, no joint definition
- **Hands/Feet:** Simple spheres, slightly flattened
- **Eyes:** Separate spheres, slightly recessed, glossy material
- **Mouth:** Texture or slight geometry indent
- **Wings:** Flat planes with slight curve, translucent material
- **Emotion ball:** Floating sphere with emissive material

### Materials:
- **Body:** Principled BSDF, high subsurface, low roughness (0.2-0.3)
- **Eyes:** Glossy BSDF, high specular, black base
- **Wings:** Translucent BSDF, slight iridescence
- **Emotion ball:** Emission shader, pulsing animation
