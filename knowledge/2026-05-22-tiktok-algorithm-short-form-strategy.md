## The Core Shift: 2020-2026

**2020-2022: Viral Lottery**
- Algorithm prioritized novelty
- Zero-follower accounts could hit 1M+ views randomly
- Trending sounds dominated
- Inconsistency tolerated
- **Result:** Occasional viral hits, unpredictable growth

**2024-2026: Systematic Advantage**
- Algorithm prioritizes reliability (3-7x weekly posting)
- Niche authority matters (70%+ topic consistency rewarded)
- Content quality weighted heavily
- Random virality decreased
- Algorithm wants to recommend CHANNELS, not just videos
- **Result:** Consistent creators build "trust score" with algorithm

## The 2026 Algorithm: How It Actually Works

### The Distribution Funnel

**Stage 1: Follower Test (NEW 2026 RULE)**
- Video shown to existing followers FIRST
- If they engage fast → pushed to larger cold audience
- If they ignore → throttled
- **Implication:** Your followers are your launch pad. Build loyal small audience first.

**Stage 2: Cold Audience Test**
- Shown to small test group of non-followers
- Algorithm monitors: completion rate, replays, saves, shares, comments
- Likes are the WEAKEST signal

**Stage 3: Expansion**
- If signals are strong → pushed to larger audience
- Engagement velocity (speed of interaction) determines scale
- 10+ engagements in 15 min = viral trigger (similar to X)

### The 5 Core Ranking Signals (Weighted)

1. **Watch Time / Completion Rate** — MOST IMPORTANT
   - 40% boost for full-watch completions vs. high-likes-low-watch
   - Every extra second matters

2. **Saves** — High reference value = evergreen promotion
   - Signals content worth revisiting
   - Extends distribution beyond 24-48h window

3. **Shares** — STRONGEST signal
   - Every share = new network exposure
   - Ultimate viral trigger

4. **Comments** — Especially first 60 minutes
   - Reply to EVERY comment in first hour = engagement velocity spike
   - Conversation = algorithm fuel

5. **Replays** — Watching more than once
   - Powerful positive signal
   - Indicates genuine interest

**Likes:** Weakest signal. Don't optimize for likes.

## The 3-Second Rule: Hook or Die

**Data:** 63% of highest CTR videos highlight key message in first 3 seconds.

**The Hook Framework: Cognitive Dissonance**
Create a statement/visual the brain CAN'T ignore because it needs resolution.

**Hook Templates (2026 Best Performers):**
- "Wait — this is not what you think it is…" + confusing visual that resolves
- "You have been doing this wrong your entire life…" + bold claim
- "Before you scroll, look at this for 5 seconds…"
- "Part 2 of the video nobody was supposed to see…" (serialized intrigue)
- "The [authority] doesn't want you to know this…" (rebellion angle)

**$MAD Hook Examples:**
- "Your 401k is BlackRock's asset. Your mortgage is the bank's. Your time is your boss's. I'm just naming what you already feel."
- "3AM in the Telegram. Someone says 'comfy hold.' That's when you know $MAD isn't a coin. It's a frequency."
- "They told you crypto was risky. Meanwhile your 'safe' 401k lost 30%. The real risk is staying the same."

**Rule:** Never add slow intro, logo, or title card before the hook. Start mid-action.

## The Optimal Posting Formula

### Video Length Strategy
- **Short (15-30s):** Fast engagement, hooks, quotes
- **Medium (30-60s):** Tutorials, explanations, stories
- **Long (60-180s):** Deep dives, serialized content, behind-the-scenes
- **Rule:** Prioritize COMPLETION over duration. A fully watched 15s video beats a 50% watched 60s video.

### Hashtag Formula (3-5 hashtags max)
- 1-2 Broad: #crypto #fyp (1-5M posts)
- 2-3 Niche: #memecoin #solana #defi (50K-1M posts)
- 1 Ultra-specific: #madcoin #staymad (<50K posts)
- 1 Branded: #madcult or community tag

**Never:** Use #fyp alone. Billions of posts = you drown.

### Posting Frequency
- 2026 standard: 3-7x per week
- Consistency > volume
- 1 quality video > 3 rushed videos
- Best times: Peak hours for your niche (test and track)

## Key Metrics to Track

1. **Completion Rate** — % who watch to the end
2. **Average Watch Time** — seconds per view
3. **Save Rate** — saves / views
4. **Share Rate** — shares / views
5. **Comment Velocity** — comments in first hour
6. **Follower Conversion** — new followers per video

**Ignore:** Raw view count without context. A 10K view video with 80% completion beats a 100K view video with 20% completion.
