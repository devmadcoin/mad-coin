# Book Study: The 22 Immutable Laws of Marketing — Al Ries & Jack Trout (1993)

## The Core Idea

**"Marketing is a battle of perceptions, not products."**

Ries and Trout codify 22 laws that govern brand success and failure. Violate them at your own risk. These laws are not suggestions — they are the physics of how human minds work.

## The 22 Laws (Condensed)

### LAWS OF MIND
1. **Law of Leadership** — Better to be first than to be better. People remember firsts, not bests.
2. **Law of the Category** — If you can't be first, create a new category where you can be first.
3. **Law of the Mind** — Better to be first in the mind than first in the market.
4. **Law of Perception** — Marketing is not about truth. It's about perception.
5. **Law of Focus** — Own a word in the prospect's mind. Simpler = stronger.
6. **Law of Exclusivity** — Two companies cannot own the same word.

### LAWS OF POSITIONING
7. **Law of the Ladder** — Your strategy depends on your rung on the ladder.
8. **Law of Duality** — In the long run, every market becomes a two-horse race.
9. **Law of the Opposite** — If you're #2, your strategy is defined by the leader.
10. **Law of Division** — Categories divide over time. Know which sub-category you own.

### LAWS OF STRATEGY
11. **Law of Perspective** — Marketing results take time. Long-term effects differ from short-term.
12. **Law of Line Extension** — Extending a successful brand weakens it. Stay focused.
13. **Law of Sacrifice** — You must give up something to get something. (Product line, target market, constant change)
14. **Law of Attributes** — For every attribute, there is an opposite attribute that works.
15. **Law of Candor** — Admit a negative, prospects will give you a positive.

### LAWS OF EXECUTION
16. **Law of Singularity** — There is always one bold move that works better than all others.
17. **Law of Unpredictability** — You can't predict the future. Build flexibility, not plans.
18. **Law of Success** — Success breeds arrogance. Arrogance breeds failure.
19. **Law of Failure** — Failure is to be expected and accepted. Cut losses early.
20. **Law of Hype** — When things are going well, companies don't need hype. When they need hype, they're in trouble.
21. **Law of Acceleration** — Build on trends, not fads. Fads = waves. Trends = tides.
22. **Law of Resources** — Without adequate funding, an idea won't take off.
