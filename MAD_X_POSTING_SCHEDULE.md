## Content Format Rules (Mobile-First Audience)

### 82% Mobile = Short + Visual

| Format | Why It Works | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Single-image meme** | Stops the scroll instantly | $MAD Mind head with text overlay |
| **Short text (under 280 chars)** | No "show more" needed | "Comfy hold. The dip is where conviction proves itself. $MAD Abundant." |
| **Screenshot drops** | Social proof + easy consumption | Telegram energy screenshot, community quote |
| **Video under 30 sec** | Auto-plays, no click needed | Roblox moment, quick affirmation |
| **Polls** | Interactive, algorithm loves engagement | "What's your $MAD affirmation today?" |

### AVOID
- Long threads (5+ tweets) unless highly engaging
- Text-only posts without visual hook
- Links that require leaving X (algorithm punishes this)
- Complex graphics that don't read on small screens
