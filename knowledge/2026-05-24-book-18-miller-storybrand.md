## The 7-Part StoryBrand Framework (SB7)

### 1. A CHARACTER
**The customer is the hero, NOT your brand.**
- Every human wakes up wanting to be the hero of their own story
- Your brand is the GUIDE, not the hero
- **Mistake 95% of brands make:** They position themselves as the hero
- **$MAD Fix:** The holder is the hero. "$MAD Rich" = their identity. We just handed them the path.

### 2. HAS A PROBLEM
**Three levels of problems:**
- **External** — "I need money, I want to invest"
- **Internal** — "I feel like I'm missing out, I want to be part of something"
- **Philosophical** — "The system is rigged, I deserve a fair shot"
- **Villain:** The rigged system, corporate extraction, financial gatekeeping

**$MAD Application:**
- External: "How do I access crypto gains?"
- Internal: "I want to feel like I belong to something real"
- Philosophical: "Why do only insiders win? $MAD changes that."

### 3. AND MEETS A GUIDE
**The guide has two qualities:**
- **Empathy:** "We understand your struggle. We've been there."
- **Authority:** "We've done this before. Here's the proof."

**$MAD Position:**
- Empathy: Doxxed dev who started from zero, not an anon whale
- Authority: Working Roblox game, real products, transparent team
- "We didn't build $MAD to extract from you. We built it to build WITH you."

### 4. WHO GIVES THEM A PLAN
**Three types of plans:**
- **Process Plan:** "Step 1: Get wallet. Step 2: Buy $MAD. Step 3: Join fam."
- **Agreement Plan:** "We promise: no rugs, no lies, no gatekeeping."
- **Post-Purchase Plan:** "Here's what to do after you hold."

**$MAD Process Plan:**
1. Create wallet (Phantom/Solflare)
2. Buy $MAD (Raydium/PumpSwap)
3. Join Telegram (community + bot)
4. Say "I'm $MAD Rich" (daily)
5. Build with us (memes, games, culture)

### 5. AND CALLS THEM TO ACTION
**Two types of CTAs:**
- **Direct:** "Buy $MAD now." (transactional)
- **Transitional:** "Learn more." (relationship-building)
- Both needed. Too much direct = pushy. Too much transitional = no revenue.

**$MAD Balance:**
- Direct: "GET $MAD" (punchy, one-line)
- Transitional: "Learn why doxxed devs matter." (education)
- The website does transitional. X posts do direct.

### 6. THAT HELPS THEM AVOID FAILURE
**What's at stake if they don't engage:**
- "Another year watching others get rich"
- "Missing the community that gets it"
- "Staying in the rigged game"
- **But don't overdo it.** Fear works short-term. Hope works long-term.

**$MAD Approach:**
- Light touch on failure. Heavy on the positive vision.
- "The cost of missing $MAD isn't just financial. It's missing the fam."

### 7. AND ENDS IN A SUCCESS
**Paint the transformation:**
- "After joining $MAD, you have a community that believes in you"
- "You say 'I'm $MAD Rich' and you FEEL it"
- "You're part of something real — not another vaporware token"
- The success is EMOTIONAL, not just financial

## KEY INSIGHTS FOR $MAD

1. **Customer is the hero, not the token** — "$MAD Rich" = their identity
2. **Three-level problems** — External (money), Internal (belonging), Philosophical (fairness)
3. **Guide with empathy + authority** — Doxxed, transparent, been there
4. **Clear process plan** — 5 steps from stranger to $MAD fam
5. **Balance direct and transitional CTAs** — X = direct, website = transitional
6. **Paint success, not just avoid failure** — Emotional transformation over financial fear
7. **One-liner clarity** — Can you explain $MAD in one breath?
