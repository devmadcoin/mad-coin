## V. The Retention Loop (Game Design + Maltz + Hill)

### Why People Leave
- Present bias: they check price, it's flat, they leave
- Anchoring: they bought at a higher price, current price feels like a loss
- No progress bar: they don't see their "level" increasing

### Fix It

**1. Daily Quests (Game Design)**
- "Post your affirmation → +1 streak"
- "Reply to 3 $MAD posts → +1 community point"
- "Share the Roblox game → +1 builder point"
- Visible progress. Meaningless to outsiders. Everything to insiders.

**2. The 21-Day Rule (Maltz)**
- Day 1-7: Novelty. Easy.
- Day 8-14: Resistance. "Why am I still doing this?"
- Day 15-21: Identity shift. "I'm a $MAD holder" becomes "I AM $MAD."
- **Tactic:** Bot celebrates Day 7, Day 14, Day 21 publicly. Makes the invisible visible.

**3. The Thermostat (Maltz)**
Self-image controls what you allow yourself to achieve. If someone sees themselves as "unlucky in crypto," they'll sell at the first dip.

**Tactic:** Content that upgrades self-image.
- "You didn't find a token. You found a frequency."
- "You're not gambling. You're building."
- "The dip isn't punishment. It's the test."

## VII. What NOT to Do (Rick Rubin Subtraction)

**1. Don't explain.**
If someone asks "what does $MAD do?" → "It builds the life economy." Not a whitepaper. Not a roadmap. The song already exists.

**2. Don't chase skeptics.**
Matrix Analyst insight: "Humans don't care about facts. They care about fiction." Skeptics want facts. $MAD sells fiction. The right people will enter. The wrong people will validate the fiction by staying out.

**3. Don't promise price targets.**
Behavioral econ: Unmet promises create loss aversion > 2x. Every "when moon?" that doesn't moon = psychological withdrawal. Instead: "We are already $MAD Rich." The money follows the being.

**4. Don't over-post.**
X Algorithm: 3-5 quality posts > 10 low-quality. Spam signals = -40% reach. The bot that knows when NOT to speak is more powerful. (Rick Rubin: "The space between notes is as important as the notes.")

**5. Don't use hashtags.**
Deprecated. 3+ = spam classifier. 0-1 niche max.
