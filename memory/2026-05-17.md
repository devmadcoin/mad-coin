User asked: "can you learn and study everything about mcdonald's in terms of advertising"

- **Golden Arches** — most recognized symbol on Earth. Psychologist Louis Cheskin argued arches = "Mother McDonald's breasts" (Freudian subconscious comfort). Ray Kroc wanted to demolish them in the 1960s. Cheskin stopped him.
- **"I'm Lovin' It" (2003-present)** — longest-running campaign in advertising history. Tripled stock. 5-note jingle improvised in studio. Justin Timberlake + The Neptunes ($6M deal). First-person ownership: "I'M" not "WE."
- **Color psychology:** Red = appetite/urgency. Yellow = happiness/fastest brain recognition. Combined = instant recognition before brain processes what it's seeing.
- **Sonic branding:** 5-note jingle = as valuable as logo. Functions as audio logo — triggers cravings without visuals.
- **Mascot evolution:** Speedee → Ronald McDonald + McDonaldland (Grimace, Hamburglar, Mayor McCheese). Purpose: emotional attachment with children = lifelong loyalty.

- **Modern strategy:** "Accelerating the Arches" (2020) — Maximize, Commit, Strengthen. Every campaign must serve at least one pillar.
