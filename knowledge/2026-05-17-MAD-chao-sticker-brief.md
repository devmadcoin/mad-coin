# $MAD Chao — 8 Sticker Pack Design Brief
## For Telegram + X Social Content

### Format Spec
- **Size:** 512×512 pixels (Telegram requirement)
- **Format:** PNG with transparent background
- **Style:** Low-poly 3D render, Sonic Chao proportions, cute/chibi, soft lighting
- **Palette:** Red (#FF0000), Black (#000000), White (#FFFFFF) ONLY. No other colors.

---

## THE 4 CHARACTERS

### 1. MAD (The Magician — Leader)
- **Body:** Round black sphere, small stubby limbs
- **Face:** Classic red $MAD face (white eyes, black pupils, slightly menacing but cute)
- **Expression:** Neutral, confident
- **Vibe:** The leader. Front and center in every group shot.

### 2. CHAOS (The Rebel — Attitude)
- **Body:** Same base mesh, black but with red asymmetrical streaks
- **Face:** Red face, one eye winking, slight smirk
- **Prop:** Optional tiny black wings (one larger than other for asymmetry)
- **Vibe:** Disruption energy. Thursday roast posts.

### 3. MIND (The Sage — Knowledge)
- **Body:** White sphere, calm/floating posture (legs tucked, sitting meditation)
- **Face:** Red face with third eye glowing white on forehead
- **Prop:** Tiny black halo or floating thought bubble with "?"
- **Vibe:** Tuesday "sharper questions" energy. Calm, sees patterns.

### 4. BAGGER (The Jester — Celebration)
- **Body:** Red sphere, black spots (like a ladybug pattern)
- **Face:** Black face with white wide grin, goofy oversized eyes
- **Prop:** Tiny black backpack or bag
- **Vibe:** "I GOT THE $MAD BAG" energy. Celebration, memes.

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES

1. **Cuteness first** — The Chao must be cute before they're edgy. Cute = shareable. Edgy = brand-aligned.
2. **Red/Black/White ONLY** — Any other color dilutes brand recognition.
3. **Simple poses** — Complex poses don't read at 512×512. Simple = iconic.
4. **Text integrated or clean** — Some stickers have text baked in, some are clean for versatile use.
5. **Transparent background** — Essential for stickers. Clean edges.
