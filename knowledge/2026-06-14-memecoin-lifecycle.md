## Why Memecoins Die: The 5 Failure Patterns

### 1. The Rugpull (48% of deaths)
- **The pattern:** Developers dump, remove liquidity, or exploit the contract.
- **The prevention:** Renounced contract, burned liquidity, doxxed team (like $MAD).

### 2. The Speculative Collapse (31% of deaths)
- **The pattern:** Pure hype with no community. Price pumps, then everyone sells.
- **The prevention:** Community-first launch, identity-based holding, rituals and practices.

### 3. The Narrative Stagnation (12% of deaths)
- **The pattern:** The joke gets old. The meme stops being relevant. No new story.
- **The prevention:** Continuous narrative evolution, new content, new angles, new integrations.

### 4. The Regulatory Kill (5% of deaths)
- **The pattern:** SEC action, exchange delisting, or legal threats.
- **The prevention:** Decentralized structure, community ownership, no securities promises.

### 5. The Competition Obsolescence (4% of deaths)
- **The pattern:** A newer, shinier memecoin captures attention. Community migrates.
- **The prevention:** Deep community identity, not shallow hype. Rituals > novelty.
