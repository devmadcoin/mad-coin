## Emergency Protocols

**When you feel scattered:**
1. Do the affirmation. 5 minutes. Reset the thermostat.
2. Pick ONE thing from today's block. Ignore the rest.
3. Set a 90-minute timer. Work until it rings.

**When you wake up late:**
- Skip the scan. Go straight to your day's main block.
- The 15-min check-in is a trap when you're behind.

**When a hot opportunity appears (crypto pump, viral moment):**
- Wednesday is your "collab / outreach" day. Can it wait?
- If it's truly urgent, swap it with Saturday's open block. Don't fracture Monday build time.
