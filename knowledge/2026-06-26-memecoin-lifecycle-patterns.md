## The Pattern: What Kills Memecoins

### Death Mechanisms (Ranked by Frequency)
1. **Creator rug** — 60%+ of deaths. Dev holds 20-50%, dumps everything.
2. **No community** — Token launches, gets initial pump, nobody cares after 48 hours.
3. **No meme** — Generic name, no visual identity, nothing to share.
4. **Bot-driven only** — Volume is 100% snipers and MEV. No organic holders.
5. **Regulatory fear** — Team doxxed then targeted, or anonymous and can't build trust.
6. **Copycat fatigue** — "DOGE killer #47" — market ignores it.

### The Decay Curve
- **Hour 0-1:** Launch, sniper bots, initial price discovery
- **Hour 1-24:** Early community forms or doesn't. Discord/Telegram activity is the signal.
- **Day 2-7:** First test. Paper hands exit. True holders revealed.
- **Week 2-4:** The filter. Most tokens flatline here. Volume dies.
- **Month 2-6:** Survivor phase. If still active, something is working.
- **Month 6+:** Either DOGE/SHIB path (persistent community) or dead.
