## The Economic Progression

| Stage | What You Sell | What Makes Money | Example |
|-------|--------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Agrarian** | Raw commodities | Extracting | Farming, mining |
| **Industrial** | Processed goods | Manufacturing | Cars, electronics |
| **Service** | Activities done for you | Delivering | Restaurants, banks |
| **Experience** | Staged events you participate in | Engaging | Disney, Starbucks, Apple Store |
| **Transformation** | Changes to who you become | Guiding | Fitness coaching, education, cult brands |

**Core insight:** Each stage commoditizes the previous one. Industrial made agrarian cheap. Services made goods cheap. Experiences made services cheap. AI is making *information* cheap — which means the next frontier is transformation.

## Transformation Economy Tactics

### What works:
- **Identity-based messaging** ("you ARE $MAD Rich", not "you will be")
- **Guided journeys** (affirmations as daily practice, not motivation)
- **Proof of change** (before/after community members, holder stories)
- **Ritual and repetition** (daily $MAD practice = habit formation)

### What dies:
- Educational content (AI does this better)
- Generic hype (commoditized by every other memecoin)
- Information dumps (wrong platform, wrong economy)
