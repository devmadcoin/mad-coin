## What to Watch For When Files Drop

### Red Flags (probably nothingburger)
- Heavy redactions (black pages)
- "Unexplained" but no extraterrestrial conclusion
- Rehashed old cases already public
- Explanations as weather balloons, drones, optical illusions

### Actual Bombshells (if real)
- Clear photos/video of non-human craft with no earthly explanation
- Biological analysis of "non-human biologics"
- Documentation of reverse-engineering programs
- Testimony from program participants (not just Grusch)
- Congressional SCIF briefings with actual evidence
- Independent scientific verification (not military assessment)

### The Middle Ground (most likely)
- Thousands of pages of "unexplained" incidents
- Confirmation that UAP are real, not misidentification
- Acknowledgment of advanced physics we don't understand
- No explicit "aliens confirmed" but enough to fuel speculation
- Continued ambiguity — "we don't know what they are, but they're not ours"
