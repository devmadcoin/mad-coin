### Key Learning
Audio synthesis is pure signal math. Every sound is just numbers oscillating at different frequencies with envelopes. The "phonk" feel comes from:
- Low bass weight (60Hz range)
- Metallic high-frequency percussion (1200-2400Hz)
- Short decay times (percussive, not sustained)
- Layering creates perceived "fullness"
