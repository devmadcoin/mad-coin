## The Hook Model: 4-Step Habit Loop

### 1. TRIGGER — The Spark
**External Triggers:** Notifications, emails, social media mentions, word-of-mouth
**Internal Triggers:** Emotions that become cues — boredom, loneliness, FOMO, excitement, anxiety
- The most powerful triggers are internal — negative emotions we want to escape
- **$MAD Application:** Daily affirmations become internal trigger. Feeling uncertain? "$MAD Abundant" replaces anxiety with conviction.

### 2. ACTION — The Behavior
**Fogg's Behavior Model:** B = MAT (Behavior = Motivation + Ability + Trigger)
- All three must converge simultaneously
- **Motivation:** Seek pleasure, avoid pain; seek hope, avoid fear; seek social acceptance, avoid rejection
- **Ability:** Six factors — time, money, physical effort, brain cycles, social deviance, non-routine
- **Core insight:** Increase ability (make it easier) more than motivation (harder to sustain)

**$MAD Application:**
- Motivation: Community identity, wealth aspiration, belonging
- Ability: One-tap buy, simple wallet setup, Telegram group for questions
- Reduce friction at every step

### 3. VARIABLE REWARD — The Dopamine Hit
**Types of Variable Rewards:**
- **Tribe** — social rewards (likes, comments, followers, validation)
- **Hunt** — material rewards (price pumps, airdrops, gains)
- **Self** — intrinsic rewards (mastery, completion, consistency — "7-day streak!")

**The Variable is Key:**
- Predictable rewards → satiation → boredom
- Variable rewards → sustained engagement → habit
- Slot machines, social media feeds, crypto prices — all variable

**$MAD Application:**
- Tribe: "$MAD fam" community validation
- Hunt: Price action, market surprises
- Self: Personal growth, affirmation streaks, knowledge accumulation

### 4. INVESTMENT — The Commitment
**What users invest:**
- Time, effort, data, social capital, emotional energy
- Investments increase likelihood of next pass through the Hook
- **Ikea Effect:** We overvalue things we partially create
- **Consistency Principle:** We act in accordance with past commitments

**$MAD Application:**
- Holders who've been in the community 30+ days have invested time, social ties, identity
- The longer they hold, the harder to sell (cognitive dissonance + investment)
- Community contributions (memes, shills, knowledge) deepen investment

## The Morality of Manipulation

**The Manipulation Matrix:**
- **Facilitator:** High ability, high motivation — you AND the user benefit (ethical)
- **Peddler:** High ability, low motivation — you benefit, user doesn't (unethical)
- **Entertainer:** Low ability, high motivation — user benefits, you don't (unsustainable)
- **Dealer:** Low ability, low motivation — nobody benefits (avoid)

**$MAD Position:** Facilitator.
- Users want financial freedom, community, identity
- We provide the vehicle ($MAD), the culture (affirmations), the belonging (fam)
- Both benefit. No extraction.

## KEY INSIGHTS FOR $MAD

1. **Make it easier, not more motivating** — Reduce buy friction, not hype harder
2. **Internal triggers > external** — "$MAD Abundant" as emotional regulation tool
3. **Variable rewards sustain engagement** — Price, community moments, surprises
4. **Investment deepens commitment** — Time in community = harder to leave
5. **The Hook works for GOOD** — Facilitator model: everyone wins
